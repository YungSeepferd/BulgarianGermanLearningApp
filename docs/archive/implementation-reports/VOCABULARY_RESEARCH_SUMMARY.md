## 1. Official CEFR Vocabulary Standards

### 1.1 German (Goethe Institut Standards)

| Level | Total Words | New Words | Exam Name | Description |
|-------|-------------|-----------|-----------|-------------|
| **A1** | ~650 | 650 | Start Deutsch 1 | Basic survival vocabulary |
| **A2** | ~1,300 | 650 | Goethe-Zertifikat A2 | Elementary communication |
| **B1** | ~2,400 | 1,100 | Goethe-Zertifikat B1 | Independent user |
| **B2** | ~3,800 | 1,400 | Goethe-Zertifikat B2 | Upper intermediate |
| **C1** | ~5,000 | 1,200 | Goethe-Zertifikat C1 | Advanced proficiency |
| **C2** | 5,000+ | Variable | Goethe-Zertifikat C2 | Near-native mastery |

### 1.2 Bulgarian (STBFL Standards)

| Level | Total Words | Exam Available | Description |
|-------|-------------|----------------|-------------|
| **A1** | ~500-700 | No official exam | Beginner (covered in early A2) |
| **A2** | ~1,200 | Yes (STBFL A2) | Basic competence |
| **B1** | ~2,000-2,500 | Yes (STBFL B1) | Threshold level |
| **B2** | ~3,500-4,000 | Yes (STBFL B2) | Vantage level |
| **C1** | ~4,500-5,500 | Yes (STBFL C1 & ECL C1) | Effective operational proficiency |
| **C2** | 5,500+ | Yes (ECL C2) | Mastery |

**Note:** The Standard Test of Bulgarian as a Foreign Language (STBFL) is conducted by Sofia University and recognized throughout Bulgaria.

## 2. Thematic Categories by CEFR Level

### A1 Level (650-750 words total)

**Core Thematic Groups (Goethe Institut):**

1. **Greetings & Farewells** (15-20 words)
   - Здравей / Hallo
   - Довиждане / Auf Wiedersehen
   - Добро утро / Guten Morgen
   - Добър ден / Guten Tag
   - Добър вечер / Guten Abend
   - Лека нощ / Gute Nacht

2. **Politeness Expressions** (10-15 words)
   - Моля / Bitte
   - Благодаря / Danke
   - Извинете / Entschuldigung
   - Съжалявам / Es tut mir leid

3. **Numbers 0-100** (100+ words)
   - Cardinals: 0-100
   - Ordinals: 1st-10th
   - Basic fractions (½, ¼)

4. **Time** (50-60 words)
   - Days of week
   - Months
   - Seasons
   - Time expressions (сега, утре, вчера)
   - Clock time (час, минута)

5. **Family** (20-25 words)
   - Immediate family (баща, майка, брат, сестра)
   - Extended family basics (баба, дядо)

6. **Body Parts (basic)** (15-20 words)
   - глава, ръка, крак, око, ухо, нос, уста

7. **Food & Drink (basic)** (40-50 words)
   - Хляб, вода, мляко, месо, риба
   - Breakfast items
   - Common fruits and vegetables

8. **Colors** (10-12 words)
   - червен, син, зелен, жълт, черен, бял

9. **Basic Verbs** (50-60 words)
   - съм, имам, правя, ходя, ям, пия
   - говоря, виждам, чувам, живея

10. **Basic Adjectives** (30-40 words)
    - добър, лош, голям, малък, нов, стар
    - топъл, студен, красив, грозен

11. **Common Nouns** (80-100 words)
    - House: къща, стая, маса, стол
    - City: град, улица, магазин
    - Nature: дърво, цвете, небе

12. **Transport (basic)** (15-20 words)
    - автобус, влак, кола, такси, самолет

13. **Directions & Locations** (15-20 words)
    - тук, там, вляво, вдясно, горе, долу

14. **Countries & Nationalities** (20-30 words)
    - България, Германия, български, немски

15. **Common Questions & Answers** (30-40 words)
    - Как си? Добре съм
    - Колко струва?
    - Къде е...?

## 3. Vocabulary Selection Criteria

### Priority Ranking System:

**Tier 1 - Essential (Must Include):**
- Frequency: 90-100
- Difficulty: 1-2
- Used in daily survival situations
- Appears in all major frequency lists
- Critical for CEFR exam success

**Tier 2 - Important (Should Include):**
- Frequency: 70-89
- Difficulty: 2-4
- Common in everyday communication
- Useful across multiple contexts
- Supports independent communication

**Tier 3 - Useful (Nice to Have):**
- Frequency: 50-69
- Difficulty: 3-5
- Specialized but valuable
- Cultural significance
- Academic or professional relevance

**Tier 4 - Advanced (Optional):**
- Frequency: 0-49
- Difficulty: 4-6
- Rare or literary
- Field-specific technical terms
- Regional or archaic

## 4. Cultural Context Integration

### Key Cultural Dimensions:

#### 4.1 Communication Style
- **Bulgarian**: More indirect, relationship-focused, emotional expression valued
- **German**: Direct (Klartext), task-oriented, emotional restraint valued

#### 4.2 Time Orientation
- **Bulgarian**: More flexible, "Bulgarian time" concept
- **German**: Extreme punctuality, planning ahead crucial

#### 4.3 Social Hierarchy
- **Bulgarian**: Respect for age and position, developing formality
- **German**: Clear professional hierarchies, formal/informal distinctions (Sie/du)

#### 4.4 Work Culture
- **Bulgarian**: Relationship-building important, flexible boundaries
- **German**: Work-life balance valued, clear professional boundaries

#### 4.5 Food Culture
- **Bulgarian**: Late dinners, extended family meals, shopska salad, banitsa, rakia
- **German**: Early dinners, Brotzeit, beer culture, Kaffee und Kuchen
